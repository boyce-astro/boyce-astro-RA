The Research Assistant is a search engine for a composite database of information from the WDS as of mid-2018, Gaia Data Release 2 (DR2), and some statistical “probabilities” of star pairs being gravitationally bound as calculated by Richard Harshaw using the DR2 data. The full DR2 portion and Harshaw calculations are displayed in SUPPORT INFORMATION. Page 1 of the file describes the algorithms devised by Richard Harshaw to determine quantitative measures of likelihoods in the database.
